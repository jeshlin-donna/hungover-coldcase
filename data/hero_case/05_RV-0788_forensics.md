in width. A consistent casting defect — a chip on ONE edge of the blade (left, relative to
the impressions) — recurs across the mark sets. The signature is distinct enough to match
a recovered tool if one is found. Photographs and silicone casts retained.

REGIONAL DATABASE CHECK is recommended to compare the 8 mm single-edge-nick signature
against burglaries in adjacent counties.
