REPORTING DETECTIVE: A. Cole, Badge 4471

NARRATIVE:
At approximately 02:40 hours, a residence at 14 Maple Heights Drive was entered without
consent. Point of entry was the rear sliding glass door, which was pried from its track.
The home office was targeted: a laptop, an external drive, and a small floor safe (~30 lb)
were removed. Entry-to-exit time is estimated at under 15 minutes based on neighbor audio.

The residence has a doorbell camera; at the time of the offense its lens was found
covered with an opaque film/spray, consistent with deliberate obstruction prior to entry.
No usable footage was recovered. No fingerprints recovered (gloves suspected).

Homeowner was away. Loss estimated at $4,200. No suspect identified at time of report.
Case status: OPEN. See forensic supplement MH-0312-FOR and witness statement MH-0312-WIT.
