partial plate beginning "8K". A DMV query on the partial plus vehicle make returned a

MH-2023-0312 (Maple Heights), AND — after a forensic examiner flagged the similarity to a
colleague — the casts from RV-2023-0788 (Riverside, Hale County). Marsh subsequently
admitted to all three burglaries.

and three families, and the link was sitting in two filing cabinets the whole time."
