INCIDENT REPORT — Case #OJ-2025-0231
Oakdale Jewelry Store Heist
Date/Time of Report: 2025-03-06 02:34
Reporting Officer: Officer P. Nakamura (Badge #5502)
Location: 22 Commerce St, Oakdale

On 2025-03-06 at approximately 02:34, I responded to a report of a break-in at 22 Commerce St, Oakdale. Upon arrival I observed signs of forced entry consistent with the use of a diamond-tip glass cutter and suction lift. Tool-mark impressions were visible on the frame and were photographed and cast for lab comparison.

The property owner/manager, Alden & Marsh Fine Jewelers (store owner: Otto Alden), was on scene and reported that several items were missing/disturbed. A vehicle matching the description 'silver Ford Transit van, plate unknown, rear bumper dent' was reported leaving the area shortly before the call came in. Canvassing of the neighborhood/area is ongoing.

Evidence collected at the scene has been logged under case #OJ-2025-0231 and forwarded to the forensics unit. This report will be updated as the investigation, led by Det. Frank Iglesias (Badge #1187), progresses. No suspects have been formally charged at this time; Renata Kovic remains a person of interest based on tool-mark and pattern similarities to related incidents in the area.
