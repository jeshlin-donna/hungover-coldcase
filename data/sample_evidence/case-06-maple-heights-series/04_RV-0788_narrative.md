NARRATIVE:
Between approximately 03:00 and 03:30 hours, a residence at 902 Riverside Lane was
burglarized. Entry was through the rear sliding glass door, forced from its track. The
home office and a bedroom closet were searched; a laptop, two external drives, and a
lockbox were taken. The household's smart doorbell recorded only a dark frame — the lens
appears to have been sprayed or covered immediately before entry.

No prints recovered; gloves suspected. Occupants asleep upstairs, heard nothing. Estimated
loss $3,600. No suspect identified.
Case status: OPEN. Forensic supplement: RV-0788-FOR. Witness statement: RV-0788-WIT.
