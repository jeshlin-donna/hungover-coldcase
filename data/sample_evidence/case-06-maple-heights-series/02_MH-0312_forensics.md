TOOL-MARK ANALYSIS:
Pry marks on the rear sliding door frame are consistent with a flat-head pry bar.
Blade width measured at 8 mm (±0.3 mm). A repeatable defect — a small nick/chip on the
LEFT edge of the blade — is impressed in three of the four mark sets, suggesting a single
tool with a distinctive signature. This signature is suitable for comparison should a
candidate tool be recovered.

TRACE: No latent prints. Glove fabric impression (smooth nitrile) on door handle.

INVESTIGATIVE NOTE: MO (rear-slider pry entry, camera obstruction, electronics + small
safe, overnight window) resembles patterns seen outside this jurisdiction.
RECOMMENDATION: cross-reference a REGIONAL / multi-jurisdiction burglary database for the
8 mm left-nick tool signature.
