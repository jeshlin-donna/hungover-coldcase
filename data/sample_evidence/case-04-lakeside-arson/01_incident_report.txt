INCIDENT REPORT — Case #LA-2025-0309
Lakeside Warehouse Arson
Date/Time of Report: 2025-03-19 03:58
Reporting Officer: Officer S. Whitlock (Badge #6120)
Location: 9 Harbor Rd, Warehouse District

On 2025-03-19 at approximately 03:58, I responded to a report of a break-in at 9 Harbor Rd, Warehouse District. Upon arrival I observed signs of forced entry consistent with the use of accelerant-soaked rags and a road-flare ignition source. Tool-mark impressions were visible on the frame and were photographed and cast for lab comparison.

The property owner/manager, Lakeside Cold Storage LLC (manager: Greta Voss), was on scene and reported that several items were missing/disturbed. A vehicle matching the description 'white pickup truck, plate BQF-118' was reported leaving the area shortly before the call came in. Canvassing of the neighborhood/area is ongoing.

Evidence collected at the scene has been logged under case #LA-2025-0309 and forwarded to the forensics unit. This report will be updated as the investigation, led by Det. Frank Iglesias (Badge #1187), progresses. No suspects have been formally charged at this time; Miguel Torres remains a person of interest based on tool-mark and pattern similarities to related incidents in the area.
